Our Story

A warm corner of Chalford worth finding.

At Lavender Bakehouse, every morning starts the same way — with fresh baking, locally sourced ingredients, and genuine care for every plate and cup that leaves our kitchen.

Whether you're joining us for a full English, a lazy brunch, a working lunch, or a proper afternoon cream tea, you'll find something made with heart. We cook from scratch, every single day.

We also offer outside catering, private party bookings, gift vouchers, and a gift shop upstairs — making us much more than just a cafe.
