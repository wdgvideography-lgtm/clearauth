Who We Are

Baking is a craft.
We take it seriously.

KNEAD is an independent bakery with one simple belief — everything tastes better when it's made properly, from the best ingredients, by people who care deeply about what they do.

We bake daily at each of our locations across the Cotswolds and Oxfordshire. Pastries, sourdoughs, seasonal patisserie, brunch. Everything made by hand, every day, without exception.

We also run supper clubs, private hire events, and coffee latte art masterclasses — because great food should be an experience, not just a transaction.
