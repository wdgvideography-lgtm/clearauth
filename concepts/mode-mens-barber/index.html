Our Story

Gloucester's finest
barbershop.

At Mode, we believe a great haircut is more than just a trim — it's a craft. Located at the entrance to Gloucester Docks on Southgate Street, we've been serving Gloucester men since day one, and we've earned every award along the way.

Our team of three highly experienced barbers bring years of dedication to every chair. Whether you're after a sharp skin fade, a classic gents cut, or a precise beard trim, you'll leave looking your best.
