Who We Are

A piece of Sydney
in the Cotswolds.

The Lynwood & Co name has been synonymous with quality for over 20 years — originally built on Australian artisan preserves and chutneys, and now carrying that same spirit into our nine cafes across the Cotswolds.

Since 2015, we've been serving properly made coffee, honest seasonal food, and the kind of warm hospitality that Australia does better than anywhere else. No pretension. Just quality.

We also offer wholesale coffee and products for local businesses that share our standards.
