About the Group

The Charles
Russell Group

The Charles Russell Transport Group has been setting standards in the transportation, lifting and handling of machinery, precision equipment and artwork since 1968. Based at Walton Hill Farm in Gloucestershire, we operate throughout the UK and internationally.

Every enquiry is handled by our experienced operations team — all with many years of on-site knowledge. From a single machine move to a complete facility relocation, our approach is meticulous, our standards are uncompromising, and our safety record speaks for itself.

What We Do

Our Services

01
Heavy Transport
Straight & step frame Euroliner curtainsiders up to 3.3m wide. Extendable semi-low trailers up to 27m. Semi-low ramp trailers for wheeled and tracked plant up to 60 tons. Extendable low loaders up to 20m. Loading by crane, forklift, side and rear access.
02
Crane & Lifting
Lorry and trailer-mounted crane vehicles capable of lifting and transporting loads up to 30 tons. HIAB vehicles, mobile cranes, Versa Lifts with fork and jib attachments. Every lift backed by SHEQ-compliant risk assessments, method statements, and lift plans.
03
Machinery Installation
End-to-end machinery handling — site visit, detailed quotation, risk assessment, execution. Gantry systems, hydraulic jacks, skates, rollers. Drilling, alignment, levelling, and assembly with OEM engineers. Single machine or complete facility relocation.
04
Warehousing & Export
HMC registered bonded warehousing serviced by our own mechanical lifting plant and overhead cranes. Secure short and long-term storage. Export project planning, specialist packing case design, container loading, and worldwide shipping co-ordination.
05
Special Projects — Art
A dedicated Special Projects Division offering global logistics and handling to the art sector. Complex art installations executed worldwide. Our highly experienced project management team has earned an enviable reputation for handling large sculptures and sensitive pieces.
06
European Logistics
Regular UK and mainland Europe routes. All tractor units carry convoi exceptionnel boards as standard. Our operations team handles all documentation, route planning, and co-ordination — giving customers a single point of contact for international movements.
