Common Questions

✦

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a Will? +
Without a Will, the law decides how your estate is distributed — which may not reflect your intentions. A professionally drafted Will ensures your wishes are clearly documented and legally binding, protecting your family and your assets.
What is a Lasting Power of Attorney (LPA)? +
A Lasting Power of Attorney allows you to appoint trusted individuals to make decisions on your behalf if you lose mental or physical capacity. There are two types: Property & Financial Affairs, and Health & Welfare. Both are essential for comprehensive future planning.
What is estate protection and do I need it? +
Estate protection strategies help safeguard your assets from potential future care costs, creditor claims or family disputes. If you own a home or have significant assets, estate protection is an important consideration to ensure what you have worked hard to build is preserved.
Do you offer home visits or online appointments? +
Yes. VC Estate Planning offers both home appointments and online consultations, making professional estate planning accessible and convenient wherever you are in the UK.
How do Trusts help with inheritance tax? +
Trusts are a powerful tool for managing inheritance tax. By placing assets into a trust, you can reduce the taxable value of your estate, ensure assets pass directly to chosen beneficiaries, and protect your family home. We advise on the most suitable trust structure for your individual circumstances.
Is the initial consultation free? +
Yes. The initial consultation with VC Estate Planning is completely free and carries no obligation. We take the time to understand your circumstances and explain your options clearly before you make any decisions.
