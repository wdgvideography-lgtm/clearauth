What We Offer

✦

How we can help you

01
Wills
Ensure your wishes are clearly documented and legally binding. Without one, the law decides how your estate is distributed — which may not reflect your intentions.
02
Mirror Wills
Ideal for couples who wish to leave their estates to each other and then to shared beneficiaries. A straightforward and cost-effective solution for families.
03
Lasting Powers of Attorney
Appoint trusted individuals to make decisions on your behalf if you lose mental or physical capacity. Both Property & Financial Affairs, and Health & Welfare types available.
04
Trusts
A powerful tool for protecting your assets and ensuring they are passed to the right people at the right time — whether protecting a family home or managing inheritance tax.
05
Estate Protection
Safeguard your assets from potential future care costs, creditor claims or family disputes. We help you put the right structures in place to protect what you have worked hard to build.
06
Probate Guidance
Compassionate, practical guidance to help executors and families navigate the probate process with confidence and clarity after a bereavement.
07
Inheritance Tax Advice
Effective estate planning can significantly reduce the inheritance tax burden on your loved ones. Clear, practical advice on structuring your estate to make the most of available exemptions.
