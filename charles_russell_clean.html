About the Group

The Charles
Russell Group

The Charles Russell Transport Group has been setting the standard in transportation, lifting, and handling since 1968. Based at Walton Hill Farm in Gloucestershire, we operate throughout the UK and internationally — built on a principle of meticulous execution, every time.

Every enquiry is handled by our experienced operations team — all with many years of on-site knowledge. Whether it's a single machine move or a complete facility relocation, our approach is thorough, our safety record exemplary, and our standards non-negotiable.
