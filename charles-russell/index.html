Our Story

BUILT ON
PRECISION &
TRUST

Founded in 1968 by Charles Russell as a single-vehicle operation, we have grown into one of the UK's most trusted specialists in heavy transport and lifting — serving industries from aerospace to fine art.

UK & European Coverage

Operating from our Gloucester base, we move cargo throughout the UK and across mainland Europe with full compliance and coordination.

SHEQ Compliant

Every project includes detailed risk assessments, method statements and lift plans prepared by our experienced operations team.

Dedicated Project Management

A designated project manager conducts a site visit for every job, ensuring your requirements are fully understood from day one.
